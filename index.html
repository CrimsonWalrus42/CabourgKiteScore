Key Recommendations

  • 6 out of 7 days are workable/good. Only Friday (onshore) is ideal for maximum wind control.
  • Best day (pure onshore): Friday 20th (S) – safest and most reliable. Wind blows from sea toward land (wind comes from water).
  • Workable days (sideshore): Sunday (NE), Tuesday (W), Wednesday (E), Thursday (NW) – wind blows parallel to beach or from sea. All are suitable for kitesurfing.
  • Avoid Monday 16th (NNW) and Saturday 21st (N) – offshore – Wind blows FROM LAND out to sea. Dangerous! Riders will be blown away from shore and rescue becomes difficult. Risk of being pushed offshore.
  • Wind speed is good on workable days (12–36 knots). The key issue is direction, not speed.
  • Simple rule: S winds = best (onshore, sea to land). E/W winds = good (sideshore). N winds = dangerous (offshore, land to sea).
